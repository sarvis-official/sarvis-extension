# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in our
community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

---

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful
- Accepting constructive feedback
- Showing empathy toward others
- Focusing on collaboration

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment or discrimination
- Trolling or insulting comments
- Public or private abuse

---

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces.
